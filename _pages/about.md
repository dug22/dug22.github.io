Here are some noteable projects of mine that I have worked on:

* [Web-Fisher](https://github.com/dug22/Web-Fisher) - a simple and comprehensive AI-powered web scraping, web application that uses Ollama, Playwright, Spring AI, and other libraries. With the scraped web content you pulled and provided, you can leverage large language models to provide insightful answers/responses to your questions/descriptions.
* [PDF Pinpoint](https://github.com/dug22/PDF-Pinpoint) - a small-scale and simple local chatbot tool powered by retrieval-augmented generation (RAG). It leverages large language models to process and train on multiple PDF documents, enabling users to efficiently find the answers they're looking for across various PDF documents.
* [Ollama Adventures](https://github.com/dug22/Ollama-Adventures) - a collection of Jupyter Notebooks demonstrating the features and capabilities of Ollama's models.
